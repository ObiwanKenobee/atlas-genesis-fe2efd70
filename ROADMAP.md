## How to Influence the Roadmap

1. **Open a GitHub Discussion** tagged `roadmap` with your proposal
2. **Submit an RFC** for significant features (see `GOVERNANCE.md`)
3. **Vote on existing proposals** with 👍 reactions on issues
4. **Contribute directly** — the fastest way to get something built

The roadmap is reviewed quarterly by the Core Team and updated based on community input, mission alignment, and technical feasibility.
