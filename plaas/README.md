---

## What This Is

PLAAS is a constellation of living intelligence systems designed to help humanity transition from **extractive civilization** to **regenerative civilization**.

Atlas Sanctum is its conscience, wisdom core, stewardship engine, memory system, and coordination intelligence вҖ” all working as one coherent planetary organism.

## Atlas Sanctum Core

The Heart, Mind, Soul, and Bridge of PLAAS:

- **Heart** вҖ” Compassion, stewardship, protection of sacred sites and communities
- **Mind** вҖ” Planetary intelligence, foresight simulation, coordination
- **Soul** вҖ” Ethics, wisdom, truth integrity, purpose alignment
- **Bridge** вҖ” Connection between communities, infrastructures, ecological systems, and future generations

---

## Architecture Principles

1. **No single point of control** вҖ” every node is sovereign
2. **Offline-first** вҖ” functions during blackouts, conflicts, infrastructure collapse
3. **Consent-based** вҖ” indigenous data sovereignty is non-negotiable
4. **Ethics before optimization** вҖ” the Temple audits everything
5. **Life over profit** вҖ” the Garden and Ark protect what cannot be bought
6. **Wisdom over data** вҖ” the Living Library synthesizes, not just stores
7. **Coordination without empire** вҖ” the Constellation connects without dominating

## Technology Orientation

| Concern | Approach |
|---------|----------|
| Sensing | IoT + satellite + community reports (Nervous System) |
| Compute | Edge AI, on-device inference, federated learning (Mycelium) |
| Networking | Mesh (LoRaWAN, libp2p, Meshtastic), offline-first (Mycelium) |
| Identity | Decentralized, wallet-based, community-sovereign (Temple) |
| Storage | Content-addressed, replicated vaults (Ark + Living Library) |
| Governance | Wisdom councils, supermajority voting, sacred vetoes (Temple) |
| Economics | Regenerative assets, local currencies, circular flows (Garden + Living City) |
| Coordination | Constellation agreements, no central authority (Constellation) |
